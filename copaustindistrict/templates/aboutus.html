About Us

The Church of Pentecost, Austin District is a branch of The Church of Pentecost U.S.A., Inc., Texas Region. The Church of Pentecost U.S.A., Inc. is the U.S.A. branch of The Church of Pentecost International which is a worldwide multi-cultural dynamic Christian group with its general headquarters in Accra, Ghana.

It traces its origin to 1937 when the Irish Missionary, Rev. James McKeown, began his ministry in the then Gold Coast (now Ghana). It is a full-gospel, Holy Spirit-filled Church carrying the gospel of Jesus Christ to all parts of the world.

The vision of the Church is to bring the world to the saving knowledge of Jesus Christ. The Church lays strong emphasis on the Word of God, holiness, prayer and evangelism. It provides leadership and discipleship to the believer to have a loving personal relationship with Jesus Christ and people. It empowers the believer to be spiritually, emotionally and physically stable and matured and to have a deeper meaning to life here on earth and hereafter in heaven as we await the Second Coming of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ.

It is our fervent believe that God can use all those who believe in Christ to achieve great things in the Kingdom of God through the power of the Holy Spirit. In this way, members are trained and encouraged to use all their spiritual gifts, talents and skills to the glory of God with each member being a vital part of the Body of Christ. It is the goal of the Church that members will have assemblies (branches) closer to them to ensure regular participation in church activities and enhance a kind of fellowship where everyone is known, loved and well cared for. In view of this, the Church is organized at different levels namely: International, National, Regional (Area), District and Local levels.

In the U.S.A., Austin District is in the Texas Region. Austin District currently has a total of four (4) assemblies namely:

- Austin PIWC and Twi  Assembly
- San Antonio Assembly 
- San Marcos PIWC
- Killeen Assembly. 

Joint services are occasionally organized.

Currently, The Church of Pentecost International is active in 109 countries across the continents.

In the U.S.A., the church membership is over 31,893 with 254 local assemblies across the States (COP USA National Report 2019).

The International Chairman of the Church is Apostle Eric Kwabena Nyamekye. The National Head for the U.S.A. is Apostle Michael Agyemang-Amoako and the Regional (Area) Head is APostle Samuel Akwesi Arthur. The District Pastor is Ovr. Gerald Hagan. The District has four Presiding Elders who oversees the various assemblies with the support of the local Presbytery. The District Pastor who is the Head of all the assemblies in the District, visits the Assembly regularly.
